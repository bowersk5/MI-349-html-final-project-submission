Propinquity Gaming

Propinquity Gaming is an indie game development studio and publisher that focuses on the casual gamer by building simple games that are easy to play and enjoy!

Gaming is no longer meant for just kids and teenagers anymore. Everyone has the potential to be a gamer! The games that we create are targeted for all ages and are lightweight builds so they are more accessible on PC's older and newer. By doing this, we are creating a space where prospective developers and new gamers can feel welcomed.
